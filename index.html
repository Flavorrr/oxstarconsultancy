About Oxstar

Established in 2012, Oxstar Consultancy Limited is a premier research and development firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, with operational reach across the region.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellent consulting firm offering innovative solutions through social and economic inclusion for sustainable development in the Horn of Africa.

Our Mission

Our core mandate is to provide evidence-based research and development solutions by utilizing innovative and scientific approaches to inform decision-making and policy formulation.
